Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Pipely ("we", "our", or "us") is a Gmail add-on that helps you manage sales contacts and deals directly from your inbox. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Access

Pipely requests the following Google permissions:

  • Gmail (read-only): To read email metadata (sender, subject, date) for creating contacts and deals. We do NOT read email body content.
  • Google Sheets: To store your CRM data (contacts, deals, settings) in a spreadsheet in YOUR Google Drive.
  • Google Tasks: To create follow-up reminders linked to your deals.
  • User Email: To identify you within team features.
Important: All your CRM data is stored in YOUR own Google Sheets, not on our servers. We do not have access to your data.

Data Storage

Pipely uses a "zero-server" architecture:

  • All data is stored in a Google Sheets spreadsheet in your own Google Drive
  • We do NOT operate any external servers or databases
  • We do NOT store, transmit, or have access to your emails or CRM data
  • The add-on runs entirely within Google's infrastructure

Third-Party Services

Pipely may optionally connect to these services if you configure them:

  • Clearbit: For company logo enrichment (only domain names are sent)
  • Hunter.io / Apollo: For contact enrichment (only email addresses are sent, if enabled)
  • Slack: For notifications (only deal summaries are sent to your webhook)

These integrations are optional and require your explicit configuration.

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for AI training
  • Access your data for any purpose other than providing the service

Data Retention

Since all data is stored in your Google Drive:

  • You have full control over your data
  • You can delete the spreadsheet at any time to remove all CRM data
  • Uninstalling the add-on removes our access but preserves your spreadsheet data

Security

  • All communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • We use Google's OAuth 2.0 for authentication
  • We request only the minimum permissions needed
  • The add-on code is executed within Google's secure Apps Script environment

Children's Privacy

Pipely is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the add-on or by updating the "Last updated" date.
